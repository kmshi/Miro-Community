Goodies | Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet allows you to add videos to your Miro Community site with one click, without having to return to your Miro Community homepage.
  1. Enable the "bookmarks" toolbar in your browser.
  2. Drag this link: Add to {{ sitelocation.site.name }} into the bookmarks toolbar. It will now become a button you can see while on any website.
  3. When visiting a webpage with a video you would like to add to your Miro Community site, simply click on the button now embedded in your bookmarks toolbar.
    A new window will pop-up with the second step of the submit process, giving you a chance to fill in any missing fields and then submit.
